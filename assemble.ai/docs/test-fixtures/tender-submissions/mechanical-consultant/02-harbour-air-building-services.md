## 1. Executive Offer

Harbour Air Building Services is pleased to submit a fee proposal for mechanical services consulting for the Lighthouse Residences apartment development.

Our proposal is based on a practical, apartment-led mechanical design approach, with careful attention to basement ventilation, natural ventilation pathways, maintainability, and coordination with the architectural and fire engineering teams.

Total fee, excluding GST: $149,500

## 3. Inclusions

The following services are included in the base fee:

- Apartment air conditioning concept, layouts and plant coordination.
- Assessment of apartment outdoor unit locations, screening constraints and condensate coordination.
- Base building mechanical ventilation to lobbies, corridors, waste rooms, plant rooms and amenities.
- Basement car park ventilation design using jet fans or ducted exhaust, subject to final structural layout.
- Basement CO monitoring system design.
- Natural ventilation review for common corridors and apartment cross-flow opportunities.
- Section J mechanical input.
- Coordination with acoustic consultant regarding major mechanical plant noise criteria.
- Attendance at design coordination meetings.
- Attendance at construction phase meetings as reasonably required within the allowance below.
- Review of contractor technical submissions and shop drawings.

## 7. Departures, Exclusions and Assumptions

1. The fee assumes the development remains a Class 2 residential building with no retail tenancy mechanical design.
2. The fee assumes one basement car park level. Additional basement levels are excluded.
3. CFD modelling is excluded unless instructed.
4. Smoke hazard management modelling is excluded and assumed by the fire engineer.
5. Acoustic calculations and plant noise certification are assumed by the acoustic consultant.
6. Mechanical services cost planning is excluded unless the adjustment above is accepted.
7. We have not allowed for redesign following major apartment mix or facade ventilation changes.
8. We assume the tenderer will provide CAD backgrounds and Revit files in a usable format.
