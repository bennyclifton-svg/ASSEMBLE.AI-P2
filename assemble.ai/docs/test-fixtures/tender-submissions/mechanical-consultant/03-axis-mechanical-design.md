## 7. Clarifications Requested

Axis requests clarification on the following items:

1. Confirm whether the basement ventilation system requires CFD modelling for authority approval.
2. Confirm whether the project includes any retail or commercial tenancy mechanical scope.
3. Confirm whether apartment AC outdoor units are permitted on balconies or must be screened in central plant zones.
4. Confirm whether CO2 monitoring is required only for common amenity spaces or also for basement support rooms.
5. Confirm the expected number of tender-stage contractor query responses.

## 8. Qualifications

1. Our offer assumes the architectural layout issued at tender remains the design basis.
2. Full smoke hazard management modelling is excluded and assumed by the fire engineer.
3. Acoustic plant noise calculations are excluded and assumed by the acoustic consultant.
4. CFD modelling is not included in the base fee and is listed as TBC.
5. Our offer includes mechanical services cost estimate input; this can be deducted if the QS is responsible.
6. If the authority requires a materially different basement ventilation strategy, redesign will be treated as an additional service.
