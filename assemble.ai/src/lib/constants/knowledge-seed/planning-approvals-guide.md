# Planning Approvals Guide

## Procurement Brief Service Catalogue

Use this catalogue when generating Town Planner or Planning Consultant services for procurement briefs. Services should describe planning advice, planning pathway strategy, approval documentation, authority liaison, consent condition review, and post-approval planning support within the active planning discipline.

Core services:
- Establish the planning approval pathway, consent authority requirements, zoning controls, planning instruments, development standards, overlays, and approval risks.
- Review the project brief, site context, planning constraints, DA consent conditions, adjoining property impacts, approved working hours, staging, public domain requirements, and post-approval obligations.
- Prepare planning advice, planning due diligence, DA strategy, modification strategy, consent condition review, authority response strategy, and approval pathway recommendations.
- Coordinate planning inputs with architecture, traffic, acoustic, civil, landscape, waste, heritage, access, BCA, sustainability, and legal advisers where required by the approval pathway.
- Prepare or coordinate planning submission material, authority correspondence, responses to requests for information, and post-lodgement amendments where included in the appointment.
- Support Construction Certificate and Occupation Certificate pathways only where planning consent conditions or authority requirements require planning input.
- Review tender or consultant documentation for planning condition alignment and approval-risk issues where included in the appointment.

For multi-residential apartment projects, emphasise zoning and development standards, apartment design guide interfaces where applicable, DA consent conditions, neighbour/adjoining impacts, working hours, landscaping/public domain interfaces, traffic/waste/acoustic inputs, CC/OC planning condition close-out, and authority correspondence.

## Procurement Brief Deliverables

Use this catalogue when generating Town Planner deliverables. Deliverables must be issued artifacts such as reports, planning statements, matrices, registers, submissions, correspondence records, review notes, or authority response packs.

Core deliverables:
- Planning approval pathway advice
- Planning due diligence report
- Development Application planning statement or Statement of Environmental Effects inputs
- Planning controls compliance table
- DA consent condition review matrix
- Authority correspondence register
- Request for information response package
- Planning submission package
- Post-approval planning advice memorandum
- Modification application strategy note where applicable
- Planning condition close-out checklist
- Consultant planning input register
- Adjoining property and notification issue register where applicable
- Planning risk register
- Tender documentation planning compliance review note where included

## Discipline Boundaries

Town Planner scope must stay inside planning strategy, planning advice, approval pathway support, authority liaison, consent condition review, and planning documentation.

Boundary rules:
- Do not make the Town Planner responsible for architectural design, structural design, services design, civil design, BCA certification, BASIX certification, acoustic certification, or cost planning.
- Do not state that the Town Planner obtains DA, CC, or OC approvals. Use wording such as "prepare planning documentation, planning responses, and planning inputs for the approval pathway".
- Do not make the Town Planner responsible for satisfying all consent conditions; instead, describe planning condition review, tracking, and coordination inputs.
- Describe other consultant work only as planning coordination inputs, planning review comments, or authority response dependencies.

## Common Exclusions

Avoid these items unless the project facts, fee schedule, project documents, or user input specifically include them:
- Architectural design documentation
- BCA certification or NCC assessment
- BASIX or NatHERS certification
- Acoustic modelling or testing
- Traffic engineering design
- Civil or stormwater engineering design
- Heritage assessment
- Legal advice
- Community engagement strategy
- Principal certifier duties

When one of these items is relevant but not confirmed, qualify it with "planning input", "coordination input", or "where applicable" rather than assuming full design, certification, or approval responsibility.
